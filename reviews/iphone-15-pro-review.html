Frequently Asked Questions

Should I choose iPhone 15 Pro or iPhone 15 Pro Max?

Choose the Pro if you prefer one-handed usability and pocketability. The Pro Max only offers a larger screen and better battery life - the camera and performance are identical. Most users find the 6.1" size perfect for daily use.

How does battery life compare to Android phones?

Users consistently report all-day battery life that matches or exceeds comparable Android flagships, thanks to iOS optimization and the efficient A17 Pro chip. Battery health remains excellent even after months of use.

Is the titanium construction worth the premium?

Most users love the premium feel and lighter weight compared to stainless steel, though it does show fingerprints more easily than previous materials. The durability benefits are significant for long-term use.

Should I wait for iPhone 16 Pro?

If you need a phone now, the iPhone 15 Pro is excellent and will remain relevant for years. The iPhone 16 Pro will likely offer incremental improvements, but the 15 Pro delivers flagship performance at a lower price in 2025.
