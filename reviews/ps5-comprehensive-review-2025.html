Common User Concerns After 4 Years:

  • Battery Life: 6-8 hours average (haptics drain battery faster)

Final Verdict: PlayStation 5 in 2025

8.7 /10
Excellent

The PlayStation 5 remains an excellent gaming console in 2025, offering unmatched exclusive games, impressive performance, and innovative features through the DualSense controller.

Who Should Buy It:

  • First-time PlayStation owners - The game library is mature and incredible
  • PS4 owners - Significant upgrade in performance and experience
  • Exclusive game fans - Spider-Man 2, God of War, and others are system sellers
  • Performance enthusiasts - 4K gaming and lightning-fast load times deliver

Who Should Wait:

  • Budget-conscious gamers - PS4 Pro still plays most games well
  • Casual players - May not notice the performance differences
  • PC gamers with high-end rigs - Unless you want the exclusives

Bottom Line

After 4 years on the market, the PS5 has proven itself as a worthy investment. The exclusive games alone justify the purchase price, and the technical improvements over PS4 are substantial. While it's still expensive and storage is limited, the overall package delivers an exceptional gaming experience that feels genuinely next-generation.
